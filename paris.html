Paris

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.
Among Paris's important museums and cultural institutions are the most visited art museum in the world, the Louvre, as well as the Musée d'Orsay, noted for its collection of French Impressionist art, and the Musée National d'Art Moderne in the Pompidou Centre,the largest collection of modern and contemporary art in Europe.The central area of the city along the Seine River is classified as a UNESCO Heritage Site;the Sainte-Chapelle (13th century);the Eiffel Tower (1889).In 2015 Paris received 22.2 million visitors, making it one of the world's top tourist destinations.
